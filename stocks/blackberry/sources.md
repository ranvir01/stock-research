## My notes on source quality

- Most useful: the 8-K/press release (hard numbers), Form 4 feed (kills the "insiders know something" theory), post-earnings analyst-target roundups.
- Hype or outdated: pre-earnings consensus aggregates ($7.58) understate post-beat targets; "AI phone" chatter is 100% noise — Clicks/Unihertz keyboard phones are third-party and pay BB nothing.
