## Recommendation (arbiter synthesis)

Both debate agents — arguing opposite sides — independently converged on "sell a chunk now, keep a runner through the Sept 24 Q2 catalyst, protect it with a stop." They differed only on the split: Bull ended at sell 25%, Bear at sell 80%. 1. **This is a loss position, not a winner.** At ~$13.07 vs a ~$15.50 basis, selling harvests a long-term tax loss (~$2.43/share sold) — the usual "don't want to pay taxes" objection to selling actually argues FOR selling here.

The runner is kept because the Bear conceded real floors (SecuSUITE through 2033, $950M QNX backlog, 5 straight profitable quarters, NVIDIA optionality) and a dated catalyst is 12 weeks away.
